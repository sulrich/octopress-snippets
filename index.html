Octopress Snippets

A number of YASnippets that make writing Octopress posts and pages easier when using Emacs.

Installation

Install these snippets under the markdown-mode snippets directory in your YASnippets directory. You can add them directly, or in their own sub-folder. Either way they will show up in a separate group under Octopress

What snippets do these install?

  • Gists
  • Inline codeblocks
  • Backtick codeblocks
  • Images
  • Blockquotes
  • Youtube videos (requires the YouTubeplugin)
  • A snippet for the 'read more' snippet.
